Setup

  1. Install the GoodThink .apk onto your Android device and open the app.
  2. Allow accessibility settings. This may require allowing restricted settings for the app.
    To allow restricted settings: tap Find out how, then go back to Settings → Apps → GoodThink, tap the three dots menu → Allow restricted settings.
  3. Allow notifications. This lets you close GoodThink from your notifications shade.
  4. Select SINGLE APP, then choose the app you'd like to use GoodThink for - for example, Instagram.

Good to know

  • Android only.
  • The screen filter covers the visibility of the buttons at the bottom of your phone (home button, etc.) - but they are still useable.
  • The screen filter won't let notifications and similar be viewed while it's open. To view notifications, go back to your home screen.
  • You can close the app by locking your phone, closing the chosen app (e.g. Instagram), or selecting Close from your notifications if allowed.
  • During setup, if entire screen is selected instead of single app, you'll get a black screen. Fix it by closing the app - just lock and unlock your phone.
